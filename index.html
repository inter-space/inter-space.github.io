About Inter Space

Inter Space Interior is registered in year 2010 to provide total solutions in interior and exterior work. The Proprietor, Mr.Mathew Tuscano has over 30 years of experience in both interior and exterior designs.

Initially begun with residential villas and flats, Mr. Mathew acquired expertise in developing commercial as well as corporate properties. Today, Inter Space Interior is an interior design studio specialized in comprehensive interiors which offer an end to end solution.

Service we offer

    Inter Space Interior is committed to provide services with highest quality standards and most value for money. We are committed to provide the following services to our esteemed clients:

  • End to end solution for interior / exterior of new houses. Our focus is on developing Luxurious, Modern and Contemporary houses. Over the last few years we have also added mid segment housing designs to our portfolio.

  • Designing and developing commercial and corporate properties. We intend to provide the robust solutions for our corporate partners in designing and developing the office / commercial / industrial space.

    Our core competencies lie with providing services like:

  • Designing
  • Flooring
  • Ceiling
  • Painting
  • Lighting / Electricals
  • Furniture
  • Plumbing
  • ACP / Glass Paneling
  • Sliding Windows and Safety Grills
  • Modular Kitchen

    • The above mentioned list is different types of work which we undertake but not limit ourselves to it. If you have any specific requirement, we strive towards providing you with our customized designs as the utmost solutions and services at your disposal.
